About this directorate

{{site_dg_long}} ({{site_dg_short}}) addresses important issues that escape the ordinary citizen's train of thought during the hustle of everyday life.

How initiatives are developed

The initiatives are brewed inside (and occasionally outside) of the head of the Commissioner of the Directorate-General. They might be the result of a sudden stroke of genius or a brilliant conversation.

Why this exists

Even though the solutions offered here might sometimes be borderline extreme, they are usually addressing real issues which might require at least second-degree thinking to be apparent. Consumers are often sold products which offer easier lives to the party buying or consuming. Unfortunately, many of these products also negate the quality of life of the unconsenting third-parties (e.g. through pollution or other harmful effects produced by the sold products).

Disclaimer

This is a fictional work of political satire. It is not affiliated with the European Union or any of its institutions. Most if not all initiatives, document codes, financial envelopes, and Directorates-General depicted on this site are imaginary.

The peer-reviewed research cited in each initiative's Scientific basis section is, however, as close to the truth as possible (given that the author is not a scientist).
