COM(2024) 0421 final Trilogue agreement reached

Automotive Emission Regulatory Simplification and Biofiltration Appliance

Repeals tailpipe emission limits in favour of mandatory in-cabin venting. Vehicle occupants form a biofiltration cohort responsible for partial absorption of internalised exhaust before ambient release.

Procedure
2024/0184 (COD)
Funding
€ 87 000 000
Lead
DG REGIO, in cooperation with DG SANTE and the automotive industry
