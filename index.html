COM(2026) 0421 final Trilogue agreement reached

Automotive Biofiltration Regulation

Repeals tailpipe emission limits in favour of mandatory in-cabin venting. Vehicle occupants form a biofiltration cohort responsible for final-stage absorption of internalised exhaust. Includes minimum-occupancy and informed-consent provisions.
